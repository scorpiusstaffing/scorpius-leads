FAQ

WHAT AGENCIES
ask first.

What about our off-limits candidates?

Your off-limits list is honoured from day one. We don't approach candidates from the firms you protect, and we map your active mandates against the target list before kickoff.

Won't candidates know it's not from our brand?

Default is co-branded outreach. We frame your agency as the senior name they should talk to, not us. White-label on retainer if you'd rather not appear at all.

Won't outbound burn our domain?

No. We use dedicated infrastructure for cold outreach. Your primary domain isn't touched. Deliverability is our problem, not yours.

How quickly can we start?

Discovery within a week. Campaign live within two. First qualified conversations on your calendar typically inside three to four weeks.
